About us

Opened in 1999, Business Center Millennium House is a Western Organization, part of the Kalchuga Fund, a Western investment fund whose properties in Moscow are rented to well-known and demanding tenants such as Deutsche Bank, Toyota Motors, Volkswagen Group Rus, Hitachi Ltd., Mitsubishi Bank of Tokyo.

Located less than 1000 m from the Kremlin, nearby Moscow best international Hotels (Marriott, Sheraton, Renaissance, Savoy, Metropol, National) and Tverskaya street, within 3 minutes walking distance from Tsvetnoy Boulevard Metro station and easy, immediate car access from the Garden Ring or the Boulevard Ring, Millennium House provides an ideal venue to develop your business successfully in Moscow and Russia.
